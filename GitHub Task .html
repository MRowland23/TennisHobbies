Tennis Hobbies Is Your Place For Tennis

Tennis is a well-renowned sport, played by millions across the globe. Tournaments are played all over the world by amateurs and pros alike. The most prestigious tournaments are known as Grand Slams (or Majors). There are 4 Grand Slams that are the most well-known, since they offer more money, media coverage and more ranking points. These 4 are the Australian Open, French Open, Wimbledon and US Open, with Wimbledon being the most famous of all Grand Slams. The 5 main events of these Grand Slams are the Gentleman’s Singles, the Women’s Singles, the Gentleman’s Doubles, the Women’s Doubles and the Mixed Doubles.
